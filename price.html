Price list

**Please Note: Price lists are current as of January 1st 2018 & can be subject to change. Products are better to be viewed in the studio so you can visually see exact sizes.**
** All prices include GST there are no hidden costs.**

Thank you for considering Jordyn Photography to capture these precious moments for you! We know that these short moments are priceless, irreplaceable, and will bring you joy every time you look at them on the wall.We offer only the best in terms of quality and longevity so we specialise in finished artwork and professional pieces.

SESSION FEE: $150

This fee is due upon booking and is a non-refundable fee to secure your booking. This fee covers the time prior, during and after your session swell as photographing your customised session.  (no products or digital files are included in this fee) Because of the custom nature of our work, our clients invest different amounts towards their sessions. Our custom wall art pieces start at $375.
Any balance obtained after your viewing session is due that day, unless a payment plan has been organised, payments can be made by direct deposit, cheque, credit card.

Products available include:

Canvas, Ice Blocks, Framing, Albums, Wall Acrylics and Portfolio Box Collections.
