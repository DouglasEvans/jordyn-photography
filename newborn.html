Newborn session information

SESSION FEE: $150

**This fee is due upon booking and is a non-refundable fee to secure your booking. This fee covers the time prior, during and after your session swell as photographing your customised session.  (no products or digital files are included in this fee) **

How exciting! Newborn photos are such an important keepsake to have as before you know it they will be running around! The best time for newborns to be photographed are up to 10 days new, yes its still so new isn't it.  The best time to book your newborn session is around 20 weeks pregnant, Paige will always try to fit an enquiry in, but she often books out well in advance so don’t leave it too late.

The reason why we photograph the new bubs up to 10 days is because they are more sleepy and most cooperative at this age so it allows us to pose them comfortably. 

Don't stress all you need to do is get your baby to us asleep and we do the rest. Paige will settle bub, wrap, position and everything- you can simply have a coffee and relax in the warm and cosy studio. 

Newborn sessions run anywhere from 60-90minutes, sometimes the bubs are so settled that they are quicker and sometimes they are a little more unsettled so we just allow that extra time incase. 

Family and sibling portraits can also be taken if you wish for no extra charge, you may even want to include your fur baby.


AFTER YOUR SESSION

After your session you will have a 10 minute coffee break while Paige selects the very best of the images to show to you. You will go through to the theatre room and view a slideshow with all your images. This is when we will go through with you to really find the ones you love and just can't live without; you only need to purchase the images that you love. All orders MUST be placed during this session so all decision makers should be present at this time. 

Minimum spend on products is $375


WHAT TO BRING TO YOUR SESSION

At Jordyn photography we have mainly everything needed for your Newborn session. We have a variety of wraps, headbands, beanies and blankets etc. One thing that we love and recommend is that you bring along any personal items, e.g. blanket made from someone special like grandma, your baby blanket, a favourite or cute teddy, any beanies or your own headbands etc. It will just make the images more personal for you.
